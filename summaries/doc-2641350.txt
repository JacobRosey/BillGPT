- **Bill Number and Title**: H.R. 100 - "No SmartPay for Anti-2A Companies Act"
- **Introduced By**: 
  - Mr. Andy Biggs
  - Mr. Scott Perry
  - Mrs. Mary Miller of Illinois
- **Date Introduced**: January 9, 2023
- **Committee**: Referred to the Committee on Oversight and Accountability
- **Main Purpose**:
  - To stop the Administrator of General Services from awarding contracts for specific commercial payment systems under the SmartPay Program.
- **Key Provision**:
  - The bill prohibits contracts for payment systems that use a payment processing agency which has a merchant category code for gun retailers.
- **Exclusion**:
  - The prohibition does not apply to any contracts that were awarded before the bill was enacted. 

This bill reflects ongoing discussions around gun rights and the financial institutions that serve the gun industry, indicating a move by some lawmakers to protect businesses related to firearms.
