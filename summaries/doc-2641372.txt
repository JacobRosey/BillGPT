- **Bill Title**: H.R. 72  
- **Purpose**: To prevent the federal government from using funds to create or maintain a database that identifies individuals who receive a COVID-19 vaccine.

- **Key Provisions**:
  - No federal funds can be spent to collect or keep information that can identify:
    - Individuals who have received a COVID-19 vaccine.
    
- **Introduced by**: 
  - Mr. Andy Biggs (Representative).

- **Committee Referral**: 
  - Referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce.

- **Important Organizations Mentioned**:
  - Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

The bill aims to address privacy concerns regarding the tracking of individuals who receive COVID-19 vaccination. This reflects ongoing discussions in society about personal medical data, individual rights, and government involvement in health matters, especially in response to the pandemic.
