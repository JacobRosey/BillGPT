- **Bill Number**: H. R. 150
- **Title**: Protecting American Energy Production Act
- **Introduced by**: 
  - Mr. Duncan (Primary Sponsor)
  - Co-sponsors include Mr. Reschenthaler, Mr. Perry, Mr. Estes, Mr. Weber of Texas, Mr. Austin Scott of Georgia, Mr. Biggs, Mr. Norman, Mr. Jackson of Texas, Mr. Carl, Mr. Stauber, Mr. Kustoff, Mr. Armstrong, and Mr. Womack.
- **Date Introduced**: January 9, 2023
- **Committees Involved**: 
  - Committee on Natural Resources 
  - Committee on Energy and Commerce
- **Main Provisions**:
  - Emphasizes that states should have the authority to regulate hydraulic fracturing for oil and gas on state and private lands.
  - Prohibits the President from declaring a moratorium on hydraulic fracturing unless it is approved by an Act of Congress.
  
This bill supports continued hydraulic fracturing practices, which are often a point of contention regarding environmental concerns and energy production in the US.
